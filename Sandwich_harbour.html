Sandwich Harbour Tour

Buckle up for an adrenaline-fueled 4x4 drive across the shifting dunes of the Namib desert, where you will witness the blazing desert dunes plunging into the cold Atlantic Ocean creating a contrast nothing short of magical. If we are lucky enough, we might stumble upon some indigenous wildlife, so keep those camera’s ready! And finally, we will arrive at Sandwich Harbor, the most important Ramsar site in Southern Africa.

What the tour offers:

  • Half Day Tour (+-5 Hours)
  • Located 80km South of Walvis Bay
  • Offroad drive along the coast
  • Exciting Dune Drive
  • Spectacular views where the ocean meets the dunes
  • Wildlife may be seen (Springbuck, Oryx, Ostrich, Birds, Whales)
  • Food and Drinks included

Combination Tours

  • Full Day Sandwich Harbor/Moonvalley Tour
  • Full Day Sandwich Harbor/Pelican Point Seal colonies
  • Full Day Sandwich Harbor/Hot Air Balloon
  • Full Day Sandwich Harbor/Catamaran Cruise
