Dune boarding

Surfing and Boogie Boarding, let's do it in the desert; the water is too cold anyway! Join us for Dune Boarding where we accommodate children, letting them slide down small safe dunes, while adrenaline junkies are taken out to some of the biggest dunes in the Namib for some fun. We will provide expert guides, so come on, let's have some fun!

What the Tour offers:

  • Short Tour (+-2 Hours)
  • In the Langstrand Dune Belt Between Swakopmund and Walvis Bay
  • Short Drive to Sliding Dune
  • Dune Boarding Experience
  • Small Picnic in Dunes
  • Food and Drinks Included
