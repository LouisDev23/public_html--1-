Spitzkoppe

Come and experience the mighty Spitzkoppe towering over the surrounding landscapes. Nicknamed the Matterhorn of Africa because of its sharp peak, Spitzkoppe also hides a lot of history. As you ascend the mountain, a local guide will share fascinating history while showing you rock drawings of the old tribes who once called Spitzkoppe their home. Enjoy the stunning views and the freedom that Spitzkoppe offers!

What the tour offers:

  • Half Day Tour (+-5 Hours)
  • Located 40km East of Swakopmund
  • Drive to Viewpoint
  • See Lichen field
  • Look at rock formations and plants
  • See Welwitschia (oldest plant in the world)
  • See the musical rocks
  • Have a nice lunch
